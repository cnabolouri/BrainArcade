Puzzle Grid

Drag cars along their allowed direction until the exit lane is clear.

Drag the red car toward the green exit on the right side.

How to Play

  • Each car can only move in its own orientation.
  • Horizontal cars slide left/right.
  • Vertical cars slide up/down.
  • Free the red car by opening a path to the exit.

Strategy Notes

  • Clear the blocking chain, not just the nearest car.
  • Use undo to test short move sequences.
  • Lower move counts mean cleaner solutions.

Session Notes

Efficient puzzle solving usually comes from identifying the deepest blocker first.
