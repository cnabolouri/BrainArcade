How to Move

  • Roll first to generate available moves.
  • Click a checker or the bar to see legal targets.
  • Green highlights show valid move destinations.
  • You must use a die if a legal move exists.

Rules Notes

Doubles give four moves. If you have checkers on the bar, you must re-enter them before moving other pieces. Bearing off follows standard legality checks.

Session Notes

Focus on escaping the back checkers without exposing too many blots.
