Runner Arena

Jump over cacti, duck under low birds, and survive longer runs.

Start the run and settle into the obstacle rhythm early.

Controls

  • Space or ↑ to jump
  • ↓ to duck while held
  • P to pause
  • R to reset

How to Improve

  • Jump early enough to land cleanly before the next cactus.
  • Don’t over-jump when the game speeds up.
  • Duck timing matters more once obstacle spacing tightens.
  • Low birds are safer to duck under than to panic-jump over.

Session Notes

The best runs come from steady rhythm, not panic reactions.
